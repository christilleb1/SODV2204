    Best Player of Calgary Flames yet

    Tkachuk became the second youngest teenager in Flames history to reach 100 games (beside Dan Quinn in 1985) when he played against the Arizona Coyotes on November 30, 2017.

    In the 2018-19 season, he was named the alternate captain of the team alongside Mikael Backlund and Sean Monahan.
